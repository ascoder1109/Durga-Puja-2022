Over here I am going to share my experience of Durga Puja pandal hopping 2022. But first if you are not familiar with Durga Puja or Pandal Hopping then let me tall you.


Durga Puja or Navratri is the biggest festival in West Bengal which happens every year in the month of October. The main reason to celebrate is becaue kit celebrates the victory of Goddess Durga over the demon king Mahishahura
